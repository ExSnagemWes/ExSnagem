Pokemon Nova Silver is a ROM hack of Pokemon Fire Red. The main principle of the game is to make the BST of all fully evolved Pokemon 530 or more, making the game able to be played through using any Pokemon you want essentially. The game also features newer generation abilities, Pokemon, moves and more! Also featured are brand-new signature moves for every Pokemon line meant to synergize with the Pokemon's abiltities, Pokedex entries, and information from other Nintendo media. This game takes place in an alternate reality. The main change? At one point long ago, a massive meteor fell onto Mt. Moon that was essentially a giant mega stone. This meteor radiated enough energy to make all Pokemon in the region and neighboring regions as well go through a large power gain akin to mega evolution. This results in a region full of Pokemon that are stronger than normal.

_______Notable Features_______

  • Physical Special Split
  • New starters of Gligar, Sneasel and Croagunk
  • All 809 Pokemon before Gen 8, as well as Gen 8 starters, Galarian and Alolan forms, Zacian, Zamazenta and Eternatus
  • Improved movepools for all Pokemon
  • Many changed abilities
  • Type-based mega stones
  • All fully evolved Pokemon have a BST of 530 or more if it was already higher
  • Trade evolution has been replaced with various evolution methods
  • New Mega Evolutions
  • HM moves are now deletable
  • TMs are now reusable
  • New TM moves
  • Many moves have been buffed
  • A few new moves
  • Many shinies have been improved or replaced (I'm looking at you, Dragonite and Kommo-o)
  • A new ability, Heavy Armor| Doubles the Defense stat
  • Reckless buffs all moves with risk including Close Combat, Overheat, Reversal, Water Spout
  • If a Pokemon has 2 forms (excluding Mega), it can switch between them with the Power Shift item
  • Trade Evolution items can now be used to activate that evolution during the battle
  • X class mid battle items will now be used if held, with a less potent effect
  • Celadon Department store now sells most items
  • Stat Boosting berries now activate at 33%
